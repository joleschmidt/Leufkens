# Product Context

## Purpose
This project serves as the digital presence for Gemeinschaftspraxis Leufkens. It addresses the need for existing and new patients to quickly find relevant information about the medical practice without barriers.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Patients (all ages) living in Sinn and surrounding areas.
- **Needs**:
  - Finding opening hours quickly.
  - Getting contact information (phone, address).
  - Understanding the range of services offered.
  - Learning about the medical team.

## User Experience Goals
1.  **Immediacy**: Key information (hours, phone) should be visible or one click away.
2.  **Trust**: The design must convey medical competence and personal care.
3.  **Accessibility**: The site must be usable by elderly patients or those with visual impairments (clear fonts, high contrast).
